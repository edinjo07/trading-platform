01 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They allow the website to recognise your device, remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the service. Cookies are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide reporting information.

Cookies can be first-party (set by TradeX Pro directly) or third-party (set by external services we use). They can be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (remain until they expire or are deleted).

02 Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies — These are essential for the Platform to function. They enable secure login, session management, and core trading features. They cannot be disabled. Legal basis: legitimate interests / contract performance.

  • tradex_session — Your authenticated session token (Supabase). Expires: session.
  • tradex_auth_token — JWT authentication token. Expires: 7 days.
  • tradex_csrf — CSRF protection token. Expires: session.

Functional Cookies — These remember your preferences and personalise your experience. Legal basis: consent.

  • tradex_theme — UI theme preference (dark/light). Expires: 1 year.
  • tradex_chart_settings — TradingView chart layout and indicator preferences. Expires: 1 year.
  • tradex_locale — Language and currency display preference. Expires: 1 year.

Analytics Cookies — We use anonymised analytics to understand how clients use the Platform and to improve it. Legal basis: legitimate interests (with opt-out available).

  • _tradex_analytics — Anonymised session and feature usage data. Expires: 90 days.

Performance Cookies — Cookies that help us measure and improve the speed and reliability of the Platform. Legal basis: legitimate interests.

Marketing Cookies — Only set with your explicit consent. Used to deliver relevant promotions to existing clients. Legal basis: consent.

03 Third-Party Cookies

The following third parties may set cookies through our Platform:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database provider. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy
  • TradingView Charting Library — Advanced charting. Cookies used for chart preferences only. Privacy: tradingview.com/privacy-policy
  • Cloudflare — CDN, DDoS protection, performance. Cookies: __cf_bm, cf_clearance. Privacy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

04 Managing & Withdrawing Consent

When you first visit the Platform, you will be shown a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by:

  • Clicking the “Cookie Settings” link in the Platform footer
  • Using your browser’s cookie management settings (see links below)
  • Contacting our DPO at dpo@tradexpro.com

Disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in and using core Platform features. Browser cookie settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

05 Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

  • Local Storage / Session Storage: Browser-side storage for Platform preferences and temporary trading state (e.g., watchlist, chart layout). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Pixel tags / web beacons: Used in transactional emails (e.g., deposit confirmation) to confirm delivery. You can disable these by setting your email client to not load external images.
