01 Who We Are

TradeX Pro Limited (“TradeX Pro”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is the data controller for personal data collected through the TradeX Pro platform and website. We are registered with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) under registration reference ZB123456.

Registered address: 22 Bishopsgate, London, EC2N 4BQ, United Kingdom. Company No. 12345678.

02 Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth, nationality, government-issued ID number, passport or driving licence images
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, postal address
  • Financial data: Bank account details, payment card details (tokenised), transaction history, deposit and withdrawal records
  • Trading data: Order history, position data, P&L records, trading preferences, risk profile
  • Technical data: IP address, device identifiers, browser type and version, time zone, operating system, access logs
  • Profile data: Username, account preferences, communication preferences, KYC/AML verification status
  • Usage data: Platform usage patterns, feature interactions, session data
  • Communications data: Records of correspondence with our support and compliance teams

03 How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Opening, maintaining, and managing your trading account
  • Executing trades and processing deposits and withdrawals
  • Complying with KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) obligations
  • Complying with regulatory reporting requirements to the FCA, CySEC, and Curacao GCB
  • Providing customer support, resolving disputes, and handling complaints
  • Sending account notifications, security alerts, and service updates
  • Improving the Platform through analytics and usage research
  • Detecting, preventing, and investigating fraud and market abuse
  • Sending marketing communications where you have given consent (you can withdraw at any time)
  • Complying with tax reporting obligations (e.g., CRS, FATCA)

04 Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK/EU GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide our trading services to you
  • Legal obligation: Processing required by FCA, CySEC, AML, tax, and other regulatory requirements
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention, IT security, improving our services, and business analytics
  • Consent: Marketing communications and non-essential cookies (you may withdraw consent at any time)

05 Data Sharing

We may share your personal data with:

  • Regulatory authorities: FCA, CySEC, Curacao GCB, HMRC, and other competent authorities as required by law
  • Banking & payment partners: For processing deposits, withdrawals, and payment card verification
  • KYC/AML providers: Identity verification and sanctions screening services (e.g., Onfido, Jumio)
  • Technology providers: Supabase (database & authentication), cloud hosting providers operating under data processing agreements
  • Market data providers: Binance, Twelve Data, CoinGecko (limited non-personal data)
  • Professional advisors: Lawyers, auditors, and accountants under confidentiality obligations
  • Law enforcement: Where required by court order or applicable law

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

06 International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers are located outside the UK/EEA. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission; the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA); or transfer to countries with an adequacy decision.

07 Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including regulatory requirements:

  • Client account data: 7 years after account closure (FCA requirements)
  • Transaction records: 7 years (AML/HMRC requirements)
  • KYC documents: 5 years after the end of the business relationship
  • Support correspondence: 3 years from date of communication
  • Marketing preferences: Until consent is withdrawn

08 Your Rights

Under UK/EU GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request)
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Right to restrict processing: Request limitation of how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability: Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Rights related to automated decision-making: Not to be subject solely to automated decisions with significant effects on you

To exercise any of these rights, contact our DPO at dpo@tradexpro.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you are not satisfied, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk.

09 Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies. Please see our full Cookie Policy for details. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie consent manager or your browser settings.

10 Children’s Privacy

Our Platform is not directed to persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at dpo@tradexpro.com and we will delete such data promptly.
